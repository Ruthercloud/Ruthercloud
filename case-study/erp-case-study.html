Custom ERP Platform Case Study

Discover how RutherCloud helped a business automate operations, centralize workflows, and improve efficiency with a custom ERP platform.

Project Overview

A growing business was managing multiple departments through disconnected systems, spreadsheets, and manual workflows, resulting in inefficiencies, data duplication, and poor operational visibility.

RutherCloud designed and developed a custom ERP platform to centralize business operations, automate workflows, and improve cross-department collaboration.

Client Challenges

  • Disconnected systems across departments
  • Manual business process management
  • Data duplication and reporting inconsistencies
  • Limited operational visibility
  • Inefficient approvals and workflow bottlenecks
  • Scalability limitations with existing tools

Our Solution

We developed a custom ERP solution tailored to the client’s operational processes and business management requirements.

  • Centralized ERP dashboard
  • Department workflow automation
  • Inventory and operations management
  • HR and employee process integration
  • Reporting and business analytics
  • Approval workflow automation
  • Role-based access and security controls

Technology Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP / Node.js, MySQL, REST APIs, Cloud Infrastructure

Key Results

  • 50% improvement in operational efficiency
  • 60% reduction in manual workflow effort
  • Improved reporting accuracy and visibility
  • Faster internal approvals and coordination
  • Scalable platform for future business growth

Business Impact

The ERP platform transformed the client’s operations by centralizing business processes, improving efficiency, reducing manual dependency, and creating a scalable digital foundation for growth.
