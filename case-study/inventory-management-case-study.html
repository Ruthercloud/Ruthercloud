Inventory Management System Case Study

See how RutherCloud helped a distribution business automate inventory tracking, warehouse workflows, and operational reporting through a custom inventory management solution.

Project Overview

A growing distribution business struggled with manual inventory tracking, stock mismatches, delayed warehouse updates, and inefficient reporting processes.

RutherCloud developed a custom Inventory Management System to centralize stock operations, automate workflows, and improve real-time visibility across warehouse activities.

Client Challenges

  • Manual stock tracking and record management
  • Frequent inventory mismatches
  • Limited warehouse visibility
  • Delayed stock updates and movement tracking
  • Inefficient reporting processes
  • Operational inefficiencies in order fulfillment

Our Solution

We designed a custom inventory management platform tailored for distribution workflows and warehouse operations.

  • Real-time stock monitoring dashboard
  • Inventory movement tracking
  • Warehouse management workflows
  • Stock alerts and low inventory notifications
  • Order and dispatch management
  • Reporting and analytics dashboards
  • Role-based access controls

Technology Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP / Node.js, MySQL, REST APIs, Cloud Infrastructure

Key Results

  • 55% improvement in inventory accuracy
  • 40% faster warehouse operations
  • 65% reduction in manual stock management effort
  • Improved reporting visibility
  • Better order fulfillment efficiency

Business Impact

The inventory management solution improved warehouse efficiency, minimized stock errors, enhanced operational visibility, and helped the business scale inventory operations more effectively.
