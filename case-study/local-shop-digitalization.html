Local Shop Digitalization

Helping a local business go digital and reach more customers.

Overview

This local retail business was operating entirely offline and relied only on walk-in customers. Due to the lack of an online presence, the shop was missing potential customers who were searching for products and services online. RutherCloud partnered with the business to create a digital foundation that would increase visibility, trust, and customer engagement without adding complexity for the owner. Our goal was to build a simple, fast, and mobile-first website that works as a digital visiting card and lead-generation tool for the shop.

Business Challenges

  • No website or online identity, making the business invisible on the internet
  • Limited customer reach
  • No digital trust or branding

RutherCloud Solution

  • Simple business website
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Contact & WhatsApp integration
  • Service and product showcase

Results Achieved

  • Increased local reach
  • More calls & enquiries
  • Business became digitally trusted
