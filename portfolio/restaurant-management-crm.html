Restaurant Management CRM

A complete restaurant management solution built to streamline billing, CRM, inventory, table operations, customer engagement, and reporting.

Project Overview

A restaurant business needed a centralized digital solution to manage daily operations including customer orders, billing, inventory tracking, staff coordination, and customer relationship management.

RutherCloud developed a custom Restaurant Management CRM platform that unified operations, improved efficiency, and enhanced customer experience through automation.

Business Challenges

  • Manual order and billing management
  • No centralized customer database
  • Inventory stock mismatches and wastage
  • Difficulty tracking sales performance
  • Inefficient staff coordination
  • Lack of customer loyalty engagement system

Our Solution

We built a custom Restaurant Management CRM designed specifically for restaurant workflow automation and customer engagement.

  • Restaurant billing & POS management
  • Customer CRM and loyalty management
  • Inventory & stock management system
  • Table reservation & order workflow
  • Staff role management dashboard
  • Sales analytics and reporting
  • Menu and pricing management
  • Cloud-based secure access

Technology Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP / Node.js, MySQL, POS APIs, Cloud Infrastructure

Key Results

  • 50% faster billing and order processing
  • Improved inventory accuracy and stock control
  • Better customer retention through CRM engagement
  • Reduced manual operational workload
  • Real-time sales reporting and performance visibility

Business Impact

The Restaurant Management CRM transformed restaurant operations by improving speed, reducing operational errors, enhancing customer relationships, and providing management with better business insights.
