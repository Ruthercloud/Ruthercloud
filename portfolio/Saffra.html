About the Project

Saffra Naturals is an Indian brand delivering pure, raw and unprocessed honey collected from diverse floral regions. Their honey is completely free from additives and preservatives, ensuring natural taste, high nutritional value and wellness benefits.

The brand offers a premium range of floral honeys including Lychee Honey with its fruity flavor and antioxidants, Jamun Honey known for its dark tone and blood sugar health benefits, and Keekar Honey from Rajasthan prized for its soothing and medicinal properties.

Saffra Naturals promotes healthy living through clean, organic and chemical-free honey sourced responsibly from trusted apiaries across India.

Key Features

  • Pure, raw and unprocessed honey
  • Free from additives, chemicals and preservatives
  • Collected from diverse floral origins across India
  • Lychee, Jamun and Keekar Honey variants
  • High antioxidant and nutritional value
  • Supports health, immunity and wellness
  • Natural flavor profiles based on floral source
  • Quality-focused and organically sourced
