Inventory Management System

A smart inventory management platform built to automate stock control, warehouse operations, order tracking, and business inventory workflows.

Project Overview

A growing business struggled with stock mismatches, manual inventory tracking, delayed stock updates, and inefficient warehouse coordination.

RutherCloud developed a custom Inventory Management System that centralized stock operations, improved inventory accuracy, and streamlined warehouse workflows.

Business Challenges

  • Manual stock and inventory tracking
  • Inventory mismatches and stock errors
  • Limited warehouse visibility
  • Delayed inventory updates
  • Inefficient order fulfillment workflows
  • Lack of reporting and operational analytics

Our Solution

We developed a scalable inventory management platform tailored to warehouse and stock management operations.

  • Real-time stock monitoring dashboard
  • Inventory movement tracking
  • Warehouse management workflows
  • Low stock alerts & notifications
  • Purchase & order tracking
  • Inventory reporting dashboards
  • Role-based secure access
  • Cloud-based business access

Technology Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP / Node.js, MySQL, Inventory APIs, Cloud Infrastructure

Key Results

  • 55% improvement in inventory accuracy
  • 40% faster warehouse operations
  • Reduced stock mismatches significantly
  • Improved inventory visibility
  • Better reporting and stock planning

Business Impact

The inventory management platform improved operational control, minimized stock-related losses, enhanced warehouse efficiency, and provided scalable infrastructure for future growth.
