Restaurant Management CRM

About the Project

RutherCloud developed a powerful and centralized Restaurant Management CRM designed to streamline daily operations for multi-location restaurants. The system helps restaurant owners manage orders, menus, customers, billing, and reporting from a single unified platform.

This solution was built to eliminate manual processes and disconnected tools by introducing a fully automated and data-driven system. It enables real-time tracking of sales, inventory, and customer interactions across all restaurant outlets.

The Restaurant Management CRM provides managers with complete visibility into operations while improving efficiency, reducing errors, and enhancing overall customer experience.

Key Features

  • Centralized restaurant management dashboard
  • Order management for dine-in, takeaway & delivery
  • Menu management with pricing & availability control
  • Customer relationship management (CRM)
  • Billing, invoicing & payment tracking
  • Real-time sales, performance & analytics reports
  • Multi-branch and multi-user support
  • Role-based access for staff and administrators
  • Automated workflows to reduce manual operations
  • Scalable system designed for single and multi-chain restaurants
