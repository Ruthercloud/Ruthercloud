Learning management system

About the Project

Global Machinery is one of the biggest global manufacturers of electronic meters with business units across India, Australia and the UK. To modernize their corporate training and e-learning process, Ruthercloud developed a fully automated Learning Management System (LMS).

This LMS is the backbone of their global training division. It manages everything from Training Needs Analysis to complete execution across all business units. The new LMS replaces the company’s legacy system with a fresh, modern, automated and intuitive platform.

The system is tightly integrated with their Talent Management and HR System, enabling seamless organization-wide training workflows. It is successfully deployed across all global locations and supports classroom training, assessments, online e-learning and various skill development programs.

Key Features

  • Fully automated LMS for corporate training management
  • Training Needs Analysis to Execution under one unified platform
  • Integrated with Talent Management & HR systems
  • Modules for classroom training, assessments & online e-learning
  • Supports interpersonal, professional & technical skill enhancement
  • Fresh, modern and intuitive user interface
  • Replaced legacy learning system with automated workflows
  • Successfully deployed across India, Australia & UK business units
