Clinic Management CRM

About the Project

RutherCloud’s Clinic CRM is a specialized system designed to manage online, web-based and face-to-face customers for clinics. It streamlines appointment handling, customer engagement, sales, and centralized product management across multiple channels.

The CRM is integrated with Sales CRM to promote and ship products sold during appointments, allowing clinics to maintain a unified ecosystem for patient interaction and product distribution.

This modern clinic CRM includes all essential modules required for managing customers, improving sales workflows, and maintaining complete visibility of clinic operations.

Key Features

  • Manage online, web, and in-person customer appointments
  • Integrated with Sales CRM for product promotion & shipping
  • Centralized dashboard for all clinic operations
  • Track products sold through multiple channels
  • Customer history & interaction management
  • Improved sales workflow for clinics
  • Modern and easy-to-use interface
