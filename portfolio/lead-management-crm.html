Lead Management CRM

A custom CRM platform built to streamline lead tracking, sales workflows, follow-ups, and customer relationship management for growing businesses.

Project Overview

A growing sales-focused business struggled with manual lead tracking, missed follow-ups, scattered customer data, and inefficient sales pipeline management.

RutherCloud developed a custom Lead Management CRM that centralized customer interactions, automated workflows, and improved sales team productivity.

Business Challenges

  • Manual lead management processes
  • Missed follow-ups and delayed responses
  • No centralized customer database
  • Weak pipeline visibility
  • Limited sales reporting insights
  • Team coordination inefficiencies

Our Solution

We built a custom CRM platform tailored to sales operations and lead lifecycle management.

  • Centralized lead management dashboard
  • Automated follow-up reminders
  • Sales pipeline tracking
  • Lead assignment automation
  • Contact & communication history
  • Sales performance reporting
  • Role-based user access
  • Cloud-based CRM accessibility

Technology Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP / Node.js, MySQL, CRM APIs, Cloud Infrastructure

Key Results

  • 45% faster lead response times
  • Improved sales conversion efficiency
  • Reduced missed follow-up opportunities
  • Better pipeline visibility and reporting
  • Enhanced sales team productivity

Business Impact

The CRM platform improved lead conversion processes, streamlined sales operations, reduced manual effort, and created a scalable foundation for customer relationship growth.
