Teleshopping CRM

About the Project

Our specialised ATS Teleshopping Enterprise CRM helps businesses avoid the complications of using multiple disconnected systems. It includes all the essential modules required to run a teleshopping operation—from call centre sales handling to receiving payments upon delivery.

The CRM also integrates with additional sales channels such as web and mobile e-commerce, enabling companies to streamline all customer interactions under one platform.

This system supports various business models including marketplace, dealer network and dropship workflows. It also includes a tightly integrated customer service module to ensure better sales support and improved customer experience.

Key Features

  • Dedicated CRM for Teleshopping business workflows
  • Call centre sales handling to COD payment management
  • Integrations with web & mobile e-commerce platforms
  • Supports marketplace, dealer and dropship models
  • Customer service integrated with complete sales process
  • Improved customer experience with smooth order handling
  • Centralised view of orders, tracking and customer history
