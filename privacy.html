Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how RutherCloud Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("RutherCloud", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our digital platforms. As an IT services and software development company, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and other details you provide through contact forms or inquiries.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and usage data.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting us via forms, email, or other communication channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our IT services and solutions
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Communicate regarding services, updates, or business inquiries
  • Enhance website performance and user experience
  • Ensure security, prevent spam, and detect fraud

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze traffic, and enhance user experience. Cookies help us understand user behavior, remember preferences, and optimize performance.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings or through our cookie consent banner. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality.

4. Google reCAPTCHA

We use Google reCAPTCHA to protect our website from spam, abuse, and automated bots. reCAPTCHA analyzes user behavior to determine whether interactions are made by humans.

This service may collect information such as IP address, device information, browser activity, and interaction data. This data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Terms of Service

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate our website and services, including:

  • Form processing services (such as Formspree)
  • Analytics tools
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Security and spam protection services

These providers only access information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated to protect your data.

6. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
