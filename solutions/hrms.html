HRMS Solution

At RutherCloud, we provide intelligent Human Resource Management System (HRMS) solutions that help businesses automate HR operations, improve employee management, and streamline workforce processes. Our HRMS platforms simplify HR tasks while improving operational visibility and efficiency.

Whether you need employee management, attendance tracking, payroll workflows, leave management, recruitment automation, performance monitoring, or HR process digitization, our HRMS solutions are designed for scalable workforce management.

Key Features of HRMS

  • Employee Management: Centralized employee records, profiles, departments, roles, and workforce organization.
  • Attendance Tracking: Automated attendance management with check-in, check-out, shift, and time tracking.
  • Leave Management: Employee leave requests, approvals, balances, and holiday management automation.
  • Payroll Management: Streamline payroll workflows, salary records, deductions, and compensation management.
  • Recruitment Management: Manage hiring workflows, applications, candidate tracking, and onboarding processes.
  • Performance Management: Employee performance reviews, KPI tracking, goal management, and assessments.
  • Employee Self-Service Portal: Allow employees to manage profiles, leave requests, attendance, and HR interactions.
  • Document Management: Secure storage for employee contracts, policies, HR records, and documentation.
  • HR Reports & Analytics: Workforce insights, attendance reports, payroll visibility, and performance dashboards.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Secure HR data access for employees, managers, and administrators.
