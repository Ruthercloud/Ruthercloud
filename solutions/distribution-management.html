Distribution Management System (DMS)

Managing distributors, inventory and order fulfillment across multiple locations can quickly become complex without the right digital infrastructure. RutherCloud’s Distribution Management System is designed to help businesses streamline distribution workflows, improve supply chain visibility and maintain better control over sales operations.

Solution Overview

Our DMS platform enables organizations to manage their complete distribution lifecycle — from order capture and stock movement to payment tracking and partner performance monitoring. With centralized dashboards and mobile accessibility, decision-makers gain real-time insights into operational performance.

The system is built to support growing distribution networks by reducing manual dependencies, improving coordination between teams and enabling data-driven planning.

Core Capabilities

  • Inventory Visibility: Monitor stock levels across warehouses and distributor locations in real time.
  • Order Lifecycle Management: Track orders from placement to fulfillment with automated status updates.
  • Distributor & Retailer Collaboration: Enable partners to place orders, track deliveries and access account information through digital interfaces.
  • Sales Performance Monitoring: Analyze regional performance trends and identify growth opportunities.
  • System Integrations: Connect with ERP, accounting platforms and analytics tools to create unified data environments.
  • Mobile Accessibility: Support field teams and distributors with mobile-friendly workflows.

Business Impact

  • Improve operational efficiency across distribution channels
  • Reduce manual coordination and data duplication
  • Enhance visibility into order fulfillment and stock movement
  • Support better credit and payment monitoring processes
  • Enable faster decision-making with centralized reporting
  • Prepare distribution networks for scalable growth

Functional Modules

  • Inventory & Warehouse Coordination
  • Sales Order & Dispatch Planning
  • Pricing, Schemes & Promotion Management
  • Distributor Onboarding & Performance Tracking
  • Field Sales Activity Monitoring
  • Payment Tracking & Credit Limit Visibility
  • Customer Support & Issue Resolution Workflows
  • Operational Reports & Executive Dashboards
