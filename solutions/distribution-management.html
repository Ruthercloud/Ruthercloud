Overview

RutherCloud DMS is a robust distribution management system designed to automate distribution processes, manage inventory, and optimize the complete supply chain. With an intuitive mobile app for distributors and retailers, businesses gain real-time visibility into orders, payments, credit limits, and stock levels.

Key Features

  • Real-time inventory tracking
  • Automated re-ordering & replenishment
  • Customizable distribution workflows
  • Advanced analytics for supply chain optimization
  • Integration with ERP, CRM & accounting systems
  • Mobile app for distributors to manage orders, payments & credit

Major Benefits

  • Real-time order and stock visibility
