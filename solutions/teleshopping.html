Teleshopping CRM & Order Management Platform

Teleshopping and call-driven commerce businesses require fast order capture, structured campaign tracking and centralized customer visibility. RutherCloud’s Teleshopping CRM framework is designed to help organizations manage high-volume enquiries, streamline order workflows and improve conversion efficiency across multiple sales channels.

Solution Overview

The platform enables businesses to manage orders received through call centers, marketing campaigns, WhatsApp enquiries, landing pages and offline sales touchpoints. With centralized dashboards and workflow automation, teams can respond faster to customer requests and maintain better operational coordination.

Built with flexibility in mind, the solution can be customized based on campaign strategy, sales process and reporting requirements.

Core Capabilities

  • Call Center CRM Workflows: Capture leads, convert enquiries into orders and manage agent productivity.
  • Campaign Performance Tracking: Monitor order sources, response trends and marketing effectiveness.
  • Order Lifecycle Management: Track orders, returns, replacements and fulfillment coordination.
  • Payment Monitoring & Reconciliation: Maintain visibility into transaction status and revenue reporting.
  • Customer Interaction History: Provide support teams with structured customer profiles and communication records.
  • Multi-Channel Sales Integration: Support enquiries from websites, messaging platforms and promotional campaigns.

Business Value

  • Improve response speed for inbound customer enquiries
  • Increase call-to-order conversion efficiency
  • Reduce manual coordination across sales and support teams
  • Gain better visibility into campaign ROI and order trends
  • Enhance customer experience through structured communication workflows
  • Enable scalable operations during high campaign demand

Functional Scope

  • Agent performance dashboards & lead queues
  • Campaign tagging & source attribution
  • Order verification & dispatch coordination
  • Return / refund workflow tracking
  • Customer support ticket management
  • Sales analytics & operational reporting
