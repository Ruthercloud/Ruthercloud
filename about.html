About Taruntelitech

Mr. Shyam Lal Teli

Founder, Taruntelitech

Mr. Tarun Teli

Director, Taruntelitech

Taruntelitech Pvt. Ltd., established in 2023, has quickly become a growing brand known for its innovative spirit and results-driven approach to software development, AI integration, and digital transformation services.

The company was founded by respected industry visionary Mr. Shyam Lal Teli, whose vision and values continue to inspire us even after his passing. Today, the company is led by Mr. Tarun Teli (Director), carrying forward his father’s dream of building future-ready digital solutions.

We’re more than just a tech company — we’re a team of passionate innovators transforming businesses through cutting-edge mobile app development, AI-powered solutions, and data analytics.

Taruntelitech’s Legacy of Innovation

From the first line of code to complex AI-driven platforms, our journey is fueled by a passion for solving real-world problems with cutting-edge technology. Every project we deliver is a testament to our innovative spirit.
