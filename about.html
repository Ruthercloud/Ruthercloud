About Ruthercloud

Mr. Tarun Teli

Founder & CEO

Ruthercloud Solutions Pvt. Ltd., established in 2023, has quickly emerged as a dynamic and innovative technology company known for its results-driven approach to software development and digital transformation.

The company was founded by Mr. Tarun Teli, who also serves as the Founder & CEO. With a strong vision for future-ready digital solutions, he continues to lead Ruthercloud toward innovation, excellence, and impactful technology.

We’re more than just an IT company — we are a team of passionate innovators dedicated to transforming businesses through cutting-edge software development, AI integration, and data analytics.

RutherCloud’s Legacy of Innovation

From building our first digital solution to delivering scalable cloud platforms and intelligent software systems, RutherCloud’s journey has always been driven by innovation. We focus on solving real business challenges using modern technologies, clean architecture, and future-ready design.

The Future is Intelligent

Technology is evolving rapidly, and so are businesses. At RutherCloud, we believe the future lies in intelligent, automated, and data-driven solutions. We integrate AI, cloud computing, and automation to help businesses stay ahead in a competitive digital world.

Our Mission

To empower startups, enterprises, and local businesses with reliable, scalable, and intelligent digital solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and long-term success.

Our Vision

To become a globally trusted software and cloud solutions company, known for innovation, quality delivery, and building long-term partnerships with our clients.
