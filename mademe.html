Mademe

About the Project

Mademe is a sustainable lifestyle brand focused on offering eco-friendly and bamboo-based personal care and home essentials. Their mission is to promote greener living by replacing single-use plastic with high-quality, durable and planet-friendly alternatives.

The brand offers a wide range of bamboo products including water bottles, charcoal toothbrushes, bamboo combs, natural loofah sponges and biodegradable earbuds. Each product is designed to be functional, long-lasting and environmentally responsible.

Mademe empowers consumers to make conscious everyday choices for a cleaner, healthier and more sustainable planet.

Key Features

  • Eco-friendly bamboo-based product range
  • Focus on reducing single-use plastic
  • Sustainable personal care and home essentials
  • Durable and high-quality materials
  • Biodegradable & planet-friendly products
  • Supports conscious and responsible living
  • Promotes greener lifestyle choices
